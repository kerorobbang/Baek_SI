About Me

I am a dedicated PhD researcher with expertise in Geomatics and Construction Management System. My research focuses on hyperspectral remote sensing, construction materials analysis, and machine learning applications in civil engineering.

Currently, I am a PhD Researcher at Pusan National University specializing in geomatics and construction management systems. I have published over 20 papers in peer-reviewed journals including Construction and Building Materials, IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing, and Korean Journal of Remote Sensing.
