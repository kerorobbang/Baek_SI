About Me

I am a dedicated PhD researcher with expertise in Geomatics and Construction Management System. My research focuses on hyperspectral remote sensing, construction materials analysis using computer vision, and marine/coastal environmental monitoring applications.

Currently, I am a PhD Researcher at Pusan National University specializing in geomatics and construction management systems. I have published 20 papers in peer-reviewed journals including Construction and Building Materials, IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing, Korean Journal of Remote Sensing, and Water.

Professional Expertise

Marine Remote Sensing

Specialized in hyperspectral and multispectral remote sensing for marine and coastal environments including red tide detection, macroalgal distribution analysis, benthic mapping, and water quality assessment using aircraft and UAV platforms.

Related Publications:
  • "A Red Tide Quantification Approach for Multispectral Camera" (2025)
  • "Review on Hyperspectral Remote Sensing of Tidal Zones" (2025)
  • "Benthic Mapping of Uljin Coast Based on Airborne Hyperspectral Imagery" (2021)
  • "Estimation of Benthic Microalgae Chlorophyll-a Concentration" (2021)
  • "Validation of GOCI-II Products in an Inner Bay" (2022)
  • "Analysis on Optical and Water Quality Measurements for Red Tide Waters" (2022)

Construction Materials Analysis

Developing computer vision and machine learning approaches for concrete aggregate property estimation and quality control using hyperspectral imaging, SAM models, and advanced image processing techniques for construction industry applications.

Related Publications:
  • "Estimation of coarse aggregate properties using hyperspectral imaging and machine learning" (2025)
  • "Evaluation of coarse aggregate properties based on segment anything model (SAM)" (2024)
  • "Hyperspectral Remote Sensing of TiO2 Concentration in Cementitious Material" (2022)

Surveying & Photogrammetry

Advanced surveying techniques using UAV-based multispectral cameras, precision improvement of exterior orientation, pixel positioning, and intercalibration methods for accurate geospatial data acquisition and processing.

Related Publications:
  • "Improving Precision of Exterior Orientation and Pixel Position of Multispectral Camera onboard a Drone" (2021)
  • "Calculation of correction coefficients for RedEdge-MX multispectral camera through intercalibration" (2020)
  • "BRDF Data for Coniferous Forests Acquired from Multispectral Camera Onboard a UAV" (2023)
  • "De-Striping and De-Smiling of Aerial Hyperspectral Image for Water Color Analysis" (2023)
