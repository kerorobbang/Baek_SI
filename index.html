About Me

I am a dedicated researcher with expertise in Civil Engineering and Remote Sensing. My research focuses on construction materials, structural health monitoring, and remote sensing applications in civil infrastructure.

Currently, I am a Research Scientist specializing in construction materials and remote sensing technologies. I have published numerous papers in peer-reviewed journals including Construction and Building Materials, Korean Journal of Remote Sensing, and Journal of the Korean Society of Civil Engineers.
